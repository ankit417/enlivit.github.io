Brand Design

Brand Design is concerned with the visual aspects of a company’s brand or identity. A company’s brand or identity can be represented in terms of design through a unique logo, or signage and is then reflected to all company materials such as business cards, packaging, media advertising and more.

Our design team can help your company build its identity. Brand design helps consumers remember the company, and often what brings them back. We realize that designers cannot make a “brand”. Only the consumers/customers can do this but the designers help in forming the foundation of the brand. This is a creative approach which takes a lot of brainstorming and practice. We believe that we’ve the right set of skills to be able to accurately reflect your company in the global market.

Here is the process we follow:

  1. Know the Client

    The client is the most important part of brand design primarily because we have to include their values and what they believe in. The elements of their culture, values and who they are should be incorporated by the brand.

  2. Know the Customers

    Knowing the client is not enough. We should be able to determine the customers and their domain. Once we get the knowledge of the audience we can morph more ideas into the brand.

  3. Know the Competitors

    Competitors are also an integral part of brand design. Knowing who is out there and how they are performing in the market is a must. This way we can set our ideas apart from the competitors which can help avoid confusions and conflicts.

  4. Know its Use

    Where/when is the brand going to be used? Our team needs to know how the brand will be broadcasted internally, externally or globally. This way we can use specific techniques when designing the brand visuals.

  5. Initial Draft

    After the requirement gathering phase we create an initial draft for the client. The initial draft will always be simple so that the client’s thinking is at our level. We focus on the task at hand and finish the draft quickly.

  6. Feedback (A/B Testing)

    Now we take the feedbacks of our client and then start tweaking things here and there as per the client. After some rounds of A/B Testing we finalize everything with the client. This is the longest phase of this process as it involves refining and changing things continuously until it’s right.

  7. Marketing

    The final step of the process in not mandatory. Some companies do this themselves. Our team can help you determine the shape, size, type of logo and title that can be used in different situations rather than using the same thing over and over again. This helps massively in marketing your product/service.
